About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and engineer specializing in renewable energy systems and agricultural robotics. My work focuses on developing practical, sustainable solutions for energy harvesting and autonomous field operations.

Currently, I'm advancing MPPT algorithms for portable solar charging systems and implementing precision agriculture robotics using ROS2 and reinforcement learning techniques.

My research integrates power electronics, control systems, and machine learning to create efficient, scalable solutions for real-world applications in renewable energy and agriculture.
