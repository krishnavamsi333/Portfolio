About Me

I am a researcher and engineer specializing in renewable energy systems and agricultural robotics. My work focuses on developing practical solutions for sustainable energy harvesting and autonomous field operations.

Currently, I am working on advanced MPPT algorithms for portable solar charging systems and precision agriculture robotics using ROS2 and reinforcement learning techniques.

My research combines power electronics, control systems, and machine learning to create efficient, scalable solutions for real-world applications in renewable energy and agriculture.
