About Me

I am a researcher and engineer specializing in renewable energy systems and agricultural robotics. My work focuses on developing practical solutions for sustainable energy harvesting and autonomous field operations.

Currently, I am working on advanced MPPT algorithms for portable solar charging systems and precision agriculture robotics using ROS2 and reinforcement learning techniques.

My research combines power electronics, control systems, and machine learning to create efficient, scalable solutions for real-world applications in renewable energy and agriculture.

Research Areas

Renewable Energy Systems

Development of advanced MPPT algorithms for solar energy harvesting, power conversion systems, and energy storage optimization for portable applications.

Agricultural Robotics

Precision farming automation using ROS2, computer vision for crop detection, and reinforcement learning for optimal harvesting strategies.

Power Electronics

Design and simulation of DC-DC converters, inverters, and control systems using MATLAB/Simulink for renewable energy applications.

Autonomous Navigation

Development of navigation systems for field robots, URDF modeling, Gazebo simulation, and real-world deployment strategies.
